APP TITLE (max 30 chars):
Galya Player – IPTV Streaming

SHORT DESCRIPTION (max 80 chars):
Stream live TV, movies & series with your own IPTV subscription.

LONG DESCRIPTION:
Galya Player is a powerful, fast, and beautifully designed IPTV player for Android. Connect your own Xtream API or M3U subscription and enjoy live TV, movies, and series — all in one app.

🎯 BRING YOUR OWN SUBSCRIPTION
Galya Player is a media player. You bring your Xtream Codes or M3U playlist — we handle the rest. No content is provided by the app.

📺 LIVE TV
Browse thousands of live channels organized by category. See what's on now with EPG program guide support.

🎬 MOVIES & SERIES
Access your full VOD library. Browse by genre, search instantly, and pick up where you left off.

⬇️ OFFLINE DOWNLOADS
Download movies and series episodes for offline viewing. Background downloads run silently with progress notifications.

🎙️ VOICE SEARCH
Find channels, movies, and series hands-free with built-in voice search.

📡 GOOGLE CAST
Stream to your TV with one tap using Google Cast support.

👤 MULTIPLE PROFILES
Create up to 5 profiles for family members. Each profile has its own watchlist, history, and settings.

🧒 KIDS MODE
Enable Kids Mode on any profile to hide adult categories and restrict content access.

🖼️ PICTURE IN PICTURE
Keep watching while browsing other apps with PiP mode.

🔒 SECURE & PRIVATE
Your credentials are encrypted and stored locally on your device. No data is shared with third parties.

⚡ FAST & SMOOTH
Optimized media player with hardware acceleration, adjustable buffer size, and subtitle support.
